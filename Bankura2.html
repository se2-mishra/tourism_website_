How To Reach
 By Flight:

Bankura does not have an airport. Nearest airport is Netaji Subhas Chandra Airport.

By Road:

There are no bus routes from other major cities to Bankura. Nearest bus stand is Durgapur.

By Train:

You can easily get regular trains to Bankura from other major cities of the country. Railway Station(s): bankura junction (BQA)
