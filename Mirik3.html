Civic Administration

The 2011 Census of India recorded a population of 11513 in Mirik Notified Area (i.e., urban unit). 5688 were males and 5825 were females.[2] Mirik rural area (under gram panchayats) recorded a total population of 46374. Males numbered 23394 and females 22980.[3] As per the 2001 Census of India,[4] Mirik urban area had a total population of 9141. Males numbered 4619 and females 4522. There is a school and a government hospital at Mirik with six general physicians and one dentist.The school is affiliated to Indian Certificate of Secondary Education.There are no specialist doctors but there is an optometrician. There are two medical shops at Krishnanagar and one pathological lab cum optical shop. There are few more medicine shops at Mirik Bazar.
