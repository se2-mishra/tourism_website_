Attractions and Activities



Baneswar Shiv Mandir, Siddheswari Kali Mandir, Rasik Bill and Madhupur, Chilapata Forest and Jayanti forest & Buxa Tiger Reserve & Fort in neighboring Jalpaiguri district.





How To Reach

Easily accessible from Kolkata and Siliguri. Bagdogra is the nearest Airport. The nearest Railway Station is New Cooch Behar.







Accomodation

‘Atithi Niwas’ run by Zilla Parishad. One can also stay at nearby Alipurduar Town (half an hour’s road journey from Cooch Behar Town) or Jaldapara Tourist Lodge run by West Bengal Tourism Development Corporation Ltd. (03563-262230) in Jalpaiguri district which is about one and half hour’s drive from the town.S
