Shopping Centres in West Bengal

This is a list of shopping malls in the Indian State of West Bengal, sortable by name, location, year opened and size (Gross Floor Area). A shopping mall or shopping center is a building or set of buildings that contain retail units and a multiplex with interconnecting walkways, enabling visitors to easily walk from unit to unit, offering diverse brands and utilities at the same place. Shopping malls became to dot the landscape of the State since the beginning of 21st century. Currently, several shopping malls are constructed in the city and many more are currently under construction or proposed.
